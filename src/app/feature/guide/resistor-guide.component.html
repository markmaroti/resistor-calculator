Band meaning

  1. First bands = significant digits.
  2. Next band = multiplier (power of ten).
  3. Final band(s) = tolerance, and optional TCR.

E-series

E-series values are preferred numbers used to standardize resistor inventories. The calculator always outputs the exact value from the chosen bands.

Tip: E12 is common, E24 is a tighter tolerance set.

Practical tolerances

For general electronics, ±5% (gold) is common. Precision circuits often use ±1% (brown) or tighter.

Temperature coefficient

TCR (ppm/°C) indicates how much the resistance changes with temperature. Lower values are more stable.
